What is it about?

PlantORLD is a company trying to educate people about houseplants in order to better take care of them. There are many different plants to choose from - we are sure there is something for everyone!

Hoya

Many different types and shapes. Some of them have beautiful colorful leaves with different patterns.

Hoya
Some of the types icludes: Hoya carnosa, Hoya Wayetti, Hoya Australis. Avoid overwatering.

Banana plant

The name says it for itself. It's a plant that originally produces fruits. Although it is not always easy (or possible) to grow bananas.

Banana plant
Banana plant does really well with bright and direct sun. During summer you can place it on the balcony or the terrace. Avoid overwatering.

Philodendron

Philodendrons are tropical plants with variety of different colors and shapes as well. Easy to grow and can be a beautiful house decoration.

Philodendron
Philodendrons enjoys indirect bright light. It will survive in places with less light although as an tropical plant, it is not ideal. Avoid overwatering.
